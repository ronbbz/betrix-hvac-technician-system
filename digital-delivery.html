Digital Delivery

How Access Is Delivered
The Betrix HVAC Technician Business System is a digital product. Access is delivered through the system dashboard after purchase and account setup are completed.
Delivery Process
After a successful purchase, access will be provided based on the checkout and account setup process in place at the time of purchase. Specific access timelines will depend on the payment and identity verification systems connected to the product.
System Updates
System updates are delivered digitally and included with your access. Updates may add new phase content, tools, templates, or system improvements over time.
Technical Issues
If you experience difficulty accessing the system after purchase, contact support for assistance.
This page contains framework-level information. Final digital delivery terms will be aligned with the checkout and access infrastructure at the time of full commercial launch.
