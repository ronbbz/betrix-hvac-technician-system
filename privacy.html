Privacy Policy

Overview
Betrix Business Systems is committed to protecting user privacy. This policy describes how information may be collected, used, and protected in connection with the HVAC Technician Business System.
Information That May Be Collected
Information may be collected through the checkout process, account setup, and system interaction. This may include name, email address, payment information (processed by a third-party payment processor), and basic usage data.
How Information May Be Used
Information may be used to provide access to the system, deliver support, improve the product, and communicate system updates. Information will not be sold to third parties.
Data Security
Reasonable measures are taken to protect user information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Users are encouraged to contact support if they have concerns about their account or data.
Contact
For privacy-related questions, contact Betrix Business Systems through the Support channel.
This page contains framework-level information. A complete privacy policy will be finalized before full commercial launch.
